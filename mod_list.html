Willatendo has a the following mods

  • End of Minecraft - Adds a new wasteland world preset to live out your wasteland fantasies!
  • Extra Item Uses - A mod that adds more functions to items!
  • Fossils and Archaeology Legacy - Adding dinosaurs since 2024! A fork of the original Fossils and Archaeology mod by Team July to the latest versions and patched up!
  • Missing Links - A mod adding stairs, slabs, walls, and other variants to blocks mojang would most likely add them too.
  • Roses - A mod for Fabric that readds some of minecraft's lost treasures! Roses and Cyan Flowers, oh my!
