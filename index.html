Why Are They Here?

Hello, if you know my mods, you'll know that they originally were on CurseForge (As well as Modrinth and Planet Minecraft with limited support). However, on February 16, 2023, CurseForge announced it's intent to include Hogwarts: Legacy mods on its site. I have pulled my mods in solidarity.

Solidarity:

To show solidarity, my mods are here, on my own website. Trans people are valid, beautiful, and important. Jewish people are also beautiful and important. Because of this, I will not alow my mods on a site willing to host a anti-semtic game from a transphobic person's stories.

The Lost Worlds

Alpha 11c for 1.16.5

Made By:

  • Willatendo
  • Dapper Dino
  • Santispik3
  • Sleepy Spino

Features:

Explore The Permian:

The last period of the Paleozoic, the Permian is a very dead area. With the combined efforts of the Carboniferous Rainforest Collapse and the combination of the two super continents, Gondwana and Laurasia lead to some of the driest time of the Earth's history. You can explore the world, just a small time before the end, when massive flood basalt eruptions in Siberia killed 81% of marine species and 70% of terrestrial vertebrate species. Find mammalian ancestors, Gorgonops, Dimetrodon.

Explore The Jurassic:

The middle of the Mesozoic, the Jurassic is a rich time era. Dinosaurs have won the race to win the game of evolution and are expanding and taking over the world! Dinosaurs spread across many new continents as Pangaea breaks apart. Explore a large lush world with some of the most famous dinosaurs!

Explore The Cretaceous:

The end of the Mesozoic, the Cretaceous is a rich time era. Dinosaurs are at the end of their time on Earth; but are thriving. Though the era will end with a meteor slamming into the Earth, there is a plethora of animals and environments to explore.

Build Your Own Park:

With the many plethora of blocks in the mod, you'll find all you need to recreate Jurassic Park and Jurassic World! Using the many soon-to-come machines, revive all of the creatures you want in your own lab!

Tips:

Recommended to use JEI with this mod.

For help, you can ask someone on the Discord Server or you can look on the wiki.

Roses

Simplified Chinese Update for 1.19.2

Made By:

  • Willatendo

Features:

Reintroduced:

Roses adds the rose back into the game! In 1.7.2, roses were replaced with rose bushes to more mick reality. But many people still love the rose and I decided to add it back in!

In the early Pocket Edition days, due to technical limitations, they could only have a certain amount of colours. As such, the Cyan Flower was introduced. Since it's so iconic, A New Leaf adds it in as well.

Chairs where supposedly originally planed due to textures found in the game code and the fact overgrown wheat would show a chair-like texture. Though likely removed to improve creativity, with Roses, you can experience them once again!

Cloth was the original name for wool! There were some unique colours that never made it to the game! Enjoy 16 cloth colours with the mod!

More Right Click Functions

Det Svenska Uppdatering for 1.19.2

Made By:

  • Willatendo

Features:

Adds the ability to break down some blocks with a pickaxe or a shovel. When right clicking on certain blocks, they will turn into others. As an example, when right clicking on a nether brick block with a pickaxe, it will turn into a cracked nether brick block. If you hit cobblestone with a shovel, you can get gravel, then sand.

Missing links

Camels and Wool for 1.19.3

Made By:

  • Willatendo

Features:

Adds a lot of missing parts of Minecraft! Stairs, slabs, and walls for a lot of blocks! Tuff, Calcite, Dripstone Blocks, and Smooth Basalt are a few examples of the blocks to get a complete new set of blocks! Smooth Sandstone gets its weirdly missing wall! All to help make Minecraft a more consistent game and to boost your creativity!
